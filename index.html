Rules

The aim of the game is to guess the two digit number.

If you get a digit right but in the wrong place, it will register as a cow.

If you get a digit correct and it is in the right place, it will register as a bull.

Click in the box to put in your guess.

Only use the onscreen buttons to show which numbers you have eliminated.
